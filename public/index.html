Go live with real data

  1. Create a free account at dashboard.api-sports.io and copy your API key.
  2. In the project, copy .env.example to .env.
  3. Paste your key into API_SPORTS_KEY=.
  4. Restart the app (npm start). The badge up top will switch to LIVE.

One key works for NFL, NBA, MLB and Soccer. The backend caches every response so you stay under the free tier’s ~100 requests/day per sport.

Edit your favorite teams and leagues in config/teams.js and .env.
