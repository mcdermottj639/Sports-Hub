About Sports-Hub

A personal hub for NFL 🦅, NBA and MLB — a daily overview of every game, an Eagles deep-dive, an AI prediction game, and fantasy tracking, all in your browser.

Live data comes from ESPN’s free public sports feed, fetched directly in your browser. No login, no API key, nothing to configure.
